Project 1 - Starship Research

This project explored the challenges and opportunities in campus delivery systems using Starship robots. Our team conducted interviews, observed real-world issues, and proposed innovative solutions to enhance efficiency and reliability. Key focus areas included:

  • User Experiences: Documented frustrations with delivery speed, app reliability, and order accuracy.
  • Design Proposals: Solutions included 'Tour Guide Starships' for campus navigation, 'Guardian Starships' with facial recognition for safety, and drone deliveries to overcome terrain challenges.
  • Technical Insights: Highlighted the need for improved routing algorithms and real-time communication to reduce delivery times.
  • Impact: Presented a vision for smarter, safer, and more efficient robot delivery networks, potentially transforming campus logistics.

Project 2 - Embodied Shopping

This project aimed to redefine the in-store shopping experience using augmented reality (AR) to enhance customer convenience and satisfaction. The project included extensive research, user journey mapping, and ideation. Key features included:

  • Personalized Shopping Experience: Smart Cart Companion concept with integrated tech to guide shoppers through stores.
  • AR Assistance: Real-time navigation, product recommendations, and instant information on promotions.
  • User-Centric Design: Focused on reducing search time, enhancing customer interactions, and improving in-store engagement.
  • Future Potential: Envisioned a seamless blend of physical and digital shopping for a more intuitive retail experience.

Project 3 - Fake Out

This project tackled the issue of 'exercise fake outs' in fitness tracking by promoting genuine physical activity over metric-driven behaviors. Our team reimagined fitness apps to foster honesty, community support, and incremental progress. Key elements included:

  • Community Focus: Emphasized peer support, motivation, and accountability through small, trusted groups.
  • Authenticity Verification: Developed AI-powered systems to detect and discourage cheating in fitness metrics.
  • Personalized Encouragement: Shifted focus from rigid step counts to more meaningful, user-centered health goals.
  • Long-Term Engagement: Designed for sustainable fitness habits, reducing anxiety and promoting real-world health outcomes.
