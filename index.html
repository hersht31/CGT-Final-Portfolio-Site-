About Me

My name is Hersh Thakkar, and I am a computer science student at Purdue University with a passion for creating innovative software solutions. I specialize in frontend design, database management, and mobile application development, blending technical skills with creative problem-solving to build impactful digital experiences. This portfolio showcases my work on projects for CGT 17208, ranging from research on the Starship robotic systems to a fitness mobile application, reflecting my commitment to user-centered design and technology-driven innovation.

Project 1 - Starship Research

This project explored the challenges and opportunities in campus delivery systems using Starship robots. My contributions included user research, design proposals, and technical insights to enhance delivery efficiency and safety.

Project 2 - Embodied Shopping

This project aimed to redefine the in-store shopping experience using augmented reality (AR). I worked on journey mapping, design ideation, and prototyping for a personalized shopping assistant.

Project 3 - Fake Out

This project tackled the issue of 'exercise fake outs' in fitness tracking. My contributions included identifying core challenges, designing community features, and developing AI-powered authenticity verification systems.
