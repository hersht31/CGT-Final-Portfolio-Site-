About Me

My name is Hersh Thakkar, and I am a computer science student at Purdue University with a passion for creating innovative software solutions. I specialize in frontend design, database management, and mobile application development, blending technical skills with creative problem-solving to build impactful digital experiences. This portfolio showcases my work on projects for CGT 17208, ranging from research on the Starship robotic systems to a fitness mobile application, reflecting my commitment to user-centered design and technology-driven innovation.

Starship Research

This project explored challenges in campus delivery systems using Starship robots. I led user interviews, concept sketches, and contributed to solution proposals like the Tour Guide and Guardian Starships. Sketches included safety and drone delivery enhancements.

  • Tour Guide Starship: Navigation and assistance for new students.
  • Guardian Starship: Safety bot with facial recognition.
  • Drone Delivery: Rapid response and terrain-agnostic drops.

Embodied Shopping

We reimagined in-store shopping using AR and a Smart Cart Companion. I prototyped journey maps, UI wireframes, and interaction flows for guiding users through aisles, surfacing relevant products, and checking inventory in real-time.

  • AR navigation and shelf scanning.
  • Inventory lookup and smart promos.
  • Personalized assistant + self-checkout concept.

Note: Visuals were created in Figma as part of our team documentation.
